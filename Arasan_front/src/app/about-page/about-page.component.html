ABOUT US

Description of the restaurant "Arasan Restaurant" in Almaty:

Welcome to Arasan Restaurant — a place where tradition meets warmth, and every meal feels like home. Located in the heart of Almaty, our restaurant is perfect for every occasion — from romantic evenings and family dinners to friendly gatherings and business lunches.

We offer two distinct spaces to host our guests: a spacious hall that can accommodate up to 100 guests for large celebrations, and a cozy hall for 30 people — ideal for more intimate experiences.

Our kitchen proudly features the rich flavors of Kazakh cuisine, from beshbarmak and kuyrdak to manty, baursaks, and regional soups. Each dish is prepared with care and deep respect for culinary heritage, using locally sourced ingredients and traditional methods.

At Arasan Restaurant, you’re not just a guest — you’re part of the family. We are committed to offering fair prices, attentive service, and a warm, inviting atmosphere inspired by Kazakh ornamentation and hospitality. Join us and experience the true taste of Kazakhstan in the comfort of our beautifully designed space!
